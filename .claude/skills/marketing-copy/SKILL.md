# Marketing Copy

## When to Use
- Writing landing page copy
- Product launch announcements
- Email campaign content
- Social media posts
- App store descriptions

## Instructions

1. Understand the product and target audience
2. Identify the key value proposition (one sentence)
3. Write in the audience's language (not technical jargon)
4. Include a clear call-to-action

## Frameworks

### Landing Page (AIDA)
- **Attention:** Bold headline that addresses pain point
- **Interest:** How the product solves it (2-3 bullet points)
- **Desire:** Social proof, features, benefits
- **Action:** Clear CTA button text

### Email Campaign
- Subject line: under 50 chars, curiosity or value
- Preview text: extends the subject line
- Body: one idea per email, scannable
- CTA: one clear action

### Social Media
- Hook in first line (stop the scroll)
- Value in 2-3 lines
- CTA or question at end
- Platform-appropriate length

## Rules
- Benefits over features ("save 2 hours/day" not "automated pipeline")
- Active voice, short sentences
- One CTA per piece
- No jargon unless audience is technical
- Include specific numbers when possible
